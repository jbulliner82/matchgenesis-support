Introduction

Match Genesis ("the App") is developed by Joseph Anton Bulliner ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Match Genesis is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not require account creation or sign-in
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information
  • We do not collect device identifiers or location data
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not integrate any analytics or advertising SDKs

Data Stored on Your Device

Match Genesis stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game Progress: Your completed levels, scores, and star ratings
  • Settings: Sound and haptic feedback preferences
  • AI API Keys (Optional): If you choose to use the AI story generation feature, any API keys you enter are stored locally on your device

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data.

AI Story Generation Feature

Match Genesis includes an optional feature that allows you to generate unique rescue scenarios using artificial intelligence. This feature:

  • Requires you to provide your own API keys from OpenAI or Anthropic
  • Makes API calls directly from your device to the AI provider
  • Does not route any data through our servers
  • Is subject to the privacy policies of the AI provider you choose to use

We never see, store, or have access to your API keys or the content generated using this feature. If you choose to use this feature, please review the privacy policies of OpenAI or Anthropic as applicable.

Third-Party Services

Match Genesis does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline (except for the optional AI feature which connects directly to your chosen AI provider).

Children's Privacy

Match Genesis does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app. All game data is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard secure storage mechanisms.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
